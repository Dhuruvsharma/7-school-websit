Academics Overview

The curriculum has been designed according to the present day’s need to find and nurture potential giftedness among those elementary students. As a co -educational English medium school affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), It has many laurels to its credit in Vasundhara viz. the first English medium school, the first to be recognized by the Government, the first to be allotted a 2 Acre regular school plot. It is also affiliated to CBSE upto XII Standard.

Accredations

The School is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) which is one of the largest educational board in the country/state. It is duly recognized by the Department of Education, Government of NCT Delhi.

Our Examination Policy

Our Examination Policy is according to CBSE guidelines for class I to XII .
