School Overview

S G Public Sr Sec School started in the modest way in Vasundhara, Ghaziabad, in the year 1992, with the noble aim of providing quality education. It is a co -educational English medium school affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) . It has many laurels to its credit in Vasundhara viz. the first English medium school, the first to be recognized by the Government, the first to be allotted a 2 Acre regular school plot. It is also affiliated to CBSE upto XII Standard.

The school is constructed on a two acre plot. In keeping with its motto "Faith and Labour" the school teaches its students to keep faith in God and put in relentless labour to achieve highest goals in their life. The school upholds secular principles. Students from different castes, communities and varying economic status are encouraged to inter -mingle harmoniously with the aim of promoting integration. Striving for excellence and perfection is a consistent feature of the school, which gives no room for complacency. Certainly no effort is spared to pace up with the changing trends of education systems and to bring our students at par with not only national but also international standards. The School is being run by Suneja Education Samiti, a registered Society comprising of distinguished educationists and dedicated persons from all walks of life.

Aims & Objectives :-

The school aims at the training of the students to become morally upright and socially useful.We are resolved to impart to young scholar such as education that promote overall development of the body, mind and soul. the school aims to blend academics with extracurricular activity, so as the student develops the qualities of selfless service to the nation, integrity, loyalty, honesty, tolerance and courage. We not only aim at making the student literate but also intermingling in them knowledge and character.
