Welcome to S G Public Sr Sec School

S G Public Sr Sec School started in the modest way in Vasundhara, Ghaziabad, in the year 1992, with the noble aim of providing quality education. It is a co -educational English medium school affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). Now School is managed by Director Raman Raja Khanna.

The school is constructed on a two acre plot. In keeping with its motto the school teaches its students to keep faith in God and put in relentless labour to achieve highest goals in their life. The school upholds secular principles. The School is being run by Suneja Education Samiti, a registered Society comprising of distinguished educationists and dedicated persons from all walks of life.
