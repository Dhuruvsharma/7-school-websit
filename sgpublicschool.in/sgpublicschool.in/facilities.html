School Facilities - Overview

The school building is well designed and provides all the facilities to the students. The classrooms are spacious, airy and adequately furnished.

The school provides excellent library & reading room facilities. It is well stocked with books covering all spheres of activities & varied tastes. The school has dedicated audio visual facilities for training students through professionally created learning aids that supplements and reinforces the conventional classroom environment. We have computer studies & computer aided learning as important part of the curriculum. The school has spacious and well equipped laboratories for Physics, Chemistry & Biology. The chemistry lab has a capacity for 50 students and has a centralized gas bank with gas burners to perform various scientific experiments. With a view to promote games sports the school have facilities for playing Football, Hockey, Skating, Throw ball, Badminton, Basketball & Cricket.

Smart Class Rooms

The school has dedicated audio visual facilities for training students through professionally created learning aids that supplements and reinforces the conventional classroom environment. All rooms are well maintained, Specious, Ventilated, Equipped with comfortable Furnitures.

School Library:

The school provides excellent library & reading room facilities. It is well stocked with books covering all spheres of activities & varied tastes. The school subscribes to a number of magazines & newspapers. Reading room facilities are provided with in the library & students are encouraged to use this facility. The students also subscribe to the H.T – Page Program to inculcate newspaper reading habit.

Computer Center:

Considering that computers are playing an increasingly significant role in the march to modernization & economic growth of the country, we have computer studies & computer aided learning as important part of the curriculum. Computer knowledge is imparted to the students from class 1st onwards. Efforts are made to enhance the sphere of knowledge of students by audio visual aids such as educational CDs etc.

Science Labs:

The school has spacious and well equipped laboratories for Physics, Chemistry & Biology. The chemistry lab has a capacity for 50 students and has a centralized gas bank with gas burners to perform various scientific experiments. Not only the senior students, but students of junior classes are also provided practical classes as a part of curriculum under the supervision of qualified and experienced science teachers.

Games and Sports:

With a view to promote games sports the school have facilities for playing Football, Hockey, Skating, Throw ball, Badminton, Basketball & Cricket. Regular periods are allotted to the students for participating in these games and sports activities.

Our sports teams frequently participate in inter school competitions within and outside the city. The school is proud to have won many of such competitions.

Transport:

The school transport is available to and from all the adjoining areas. Students using buses will be picked up and dropped only at the stops fixed by school.

Purified Mineral Water:

Our school gives an ultimate attention regarding health, for that our school paying attention towards drinking water. Our school is equipped with RO plant that removes the Total Dissolved Salts (TDS), in addition to physical, chemical and microbiological contaminants.

Campus Security:

The building has two alternative stairs. There are three alternative exits with a main entrance which secure life in case of emergency vacant. The school's tight security makes our students feel safe and sound.

Theatre, Music lab, Dance Room, Arts & Crafts and Pottery
