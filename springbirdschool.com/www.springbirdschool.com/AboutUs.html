Introduction About Our School

Founded in 2009, by the Springbird Educational Society in the heart of Sonipat, Haryana, Sprinbird School prepares students for their life goals and targets. The beautiful building with its ample grounds is equipped with naturally well-lit and ventilated classrooms provide an ideal setting for academic rigour and an all – round personality development.

Springbird Sr. Sec. School, Sonipat is a premier day-cum-boarding school offering a world class campus in close proximity to Delhi. The school offers an complete platform for its students with a perfect blend of technology, modern pedagogy, culture and innovation. Our School is affiliated to CBSE. The curriculum is comprehensive and based on core subjects going beyond academic book knowledge but also facilitating development of concepts, ideas and skills. Springbird provides novel sports facilities and plethora of co-curricular activitie. It offers state-of-the-art boarding facilities which include extended day school up to 4 pm (day-boarding).Springbird believes that learning is a dynamic process where each student learns differently. We provide developmentally appropriate practices by teaching in ways that match the way students develop and learn. We provide daily experiences to students for the independently discover that they are competent and capable learners
Springbird School is situated in Sonipat, and is part of the National Capital Region (Delhi NCR). The campus was designed by an architectural firm. A Wi-Fi enabled campus, complete with spacious classrooms, multimedia hall, library, computerised labs and classrooms; form the backbone of the academic infrastructure, which is at par with international standards. Springbird prides itself on providing a fully air- conditioned multi sports indoor hall, a separate toddler's pool, three sports fields, badminton, basketball courts, and archery arena.
24/7 security of the campus is ensured through strategically located surveillance cameras and CCTV monitoring by trained security personnel. Highly trained security officers from CRPF and security agencies like Infinity Security Management Services, have been appointed to further tighten the security. Students and teachers are granted access through smart cards to ensure improved security network and authentication. The green campus concept has been employed in planning and construction of the school. The highlights of this approach include the use of fly ash in building material, solar heating, water harvesting, recycling of waste, effluent treatment, plastic free and chemical free landscaping. Bio-waste management is being actively considered at the moment. The transport facilities provided by the school includes buses that are technically equipped with a Vehicle Tracking System (VTS) that advocates the security staff in tracing the bus route whenever needed thereby ensuring the safety and security of students. Each bus carries a caretaker to pick and drop children safely from their designated bus stops to school and back. We are providing transport from all over the city.
