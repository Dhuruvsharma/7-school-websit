About SBS

Springbird School (SBS), Sonipat Founded in 2009, by the Springbird Educational Society in the heart of Sonipat, Haryana, Sprinbird School prepares students for their life goals and targets. The beautiful building with its ample grounds is equipped with naturally well-lit and ventilated classrooms provide an ideal setting for academic rigour and an all – round personality development.

TOP INFRASTRUCTURE AND FACILITIES

TRANSPORT

The school has an extensive fleet of well maintained buses which cover a large radius around the school. All our buses are manned by qualified and experienced drivers.

TABLE TENNIS

The school has its well furnished table tennis hall and a world class wooden Badminton Court where Under the guidance of qualified coaches, students polish their sporting talent.

LIBRARY

A library can rightly be called the reservoir of knowledge. We have a very well established and fully computerized library which is updated every year.

SCIENCE AND MATHS LAB

The Science and Maths Laboratories are furnished with adequate equipments that enable the budding stalwarts conduct the experiments with active participation.

MUSIC & DANCE

Students are exposed to music, dance, music instrument learning and other co-curricular activities throughout the year.

BUILDING

The school offers an complete platform for its students with a perfect blend of technology, modern pedagogy, culture and innovation. The curriculum is comprehensive and based on core subjects going beyond academic book knowledge but also facilitating development of concepts, ideas and skills.
