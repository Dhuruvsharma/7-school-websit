Welcome to
Aryan Public School

APS emerged on 21st February 2000.The foundation stone was laid by Shri B.M Goel (chairperson). It is a co-educational institution run by Sanjeevani Siksha Samiti a non-profit organization registered under the Societies Act-1861.

The educational foundation is the umbrella body for Aryan Schools and is committed to do philanthropic jobs and is the driving sprit and aims at turning it into a beacon of excellence for quality education. The managing committee of the society includes prominent business personnel and social workers.
